WARMWU Galvanized Steel Track Roller for Sliding Gates

Upgrade your sliding gate with the WARMWU Track Rollers, designed to blend durability, reliability, and precision in every detail. These essential components are made from high-quality, hot-dipped galvanized steel, ensuring they remain strong and resistant to wear no matter the conditions.

Whether you're building a new gate or enhancing an existing one, these rollers are built to handle it all. Their heavy-duty construction and rust-resistant finish make them perfect for use in both extreme weather and long-term outdoor settings, delivering dependable performance year-round.

The universal fit for 2" (1-7/8") gate frames and 1-5/8" track pipes ensures compatibility with a wide range of gate systems, making installation and maintenance simpler than ever. With a double U bolt design, these rollers provide added stability and security to your gate, preventing misalignment and ensuring smooth operation.

Featuring a 5" wheel size, these track rollers offer silent and efficient movement, keeping your gate functioning like new without unnecessary noise. A smart choice for any driveway, garage, or property, they enhance safety and convenience, standing the test of time with every use.
