Soft and Breathable Material

Crafted from 95% organic cotton and 5% elastane, this sleep sack is gentle on your baby's delicate skin and offers a flexible fit that moves with them.

The wide waist design allows natural leg spread, supporting healthy hip development and helping to prevent hip dysplasia in early infancy.

Arms Up Design for Better Sleep

This transition swaddle is perfect for newborns and babies up to 6 months old. It allows gradual movement from arms in to arms out, helping your baby adjust as they grow.
